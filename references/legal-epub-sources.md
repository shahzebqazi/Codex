# Legal EPUB Sources (Vetted)

Retrieved: 2026-02-12

## Verification Rules

- Keep only books with explicit legal basis: public domain, Creative Commons, or other open legal terms.
- Save one proof URL per source.
- If rights are regional (for example public domain in US only), mark the region.

## Source 1: Project Gutenberg (Public Domain)

- Home: [Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/)
- Rights proof: [Copyright How-To](https://www.gutenberg.org/help/copyright.html)
- Permission/legal terms: [Permission, Copyright, Licensing](https://www.gutenberg.org/policy/permission.html)
- Notes: Public-domain focus; verify local-country copyright status when outside US.

- Notes: Directory criteria require open licensing and downloadable portable files.

Starter EPUB-capable textbooks:

- [Communication in the Real World (Pressbooks)](https://openoregon.pressbooks.pub/commrealworld/)

## Rejection Rules

Reject a source when:

- License is missing or unclear.
- Terms prohibit download/redistribution needed by the user.
- Site appears to mirror copyrighted books without legal permission.
